# About Frustrated Citizen

Welcome. If you are here, you are probably tired of the same old narratives. 

I created **Frustrated Citizen** to question systems that are fundamentally broken—like our education evaluation boards, paper leaks, and the illusion of meritocracy. This is a space for raw, unfiltered reality about the issues ordinary people face every day.

Stick around. We have a lot to talk about.
